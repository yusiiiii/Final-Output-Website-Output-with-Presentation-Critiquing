Algorithms and Data Structures

User: What are Algorithms and Data Structures?
Algorithms are step-by-step procedures for solving problems; data structures are ways to organize and store data efficiently. Examples: arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs. Algorithms include sorting, searching, graph traversal, and dynamic programming. Together they determine the efficiency and scalability of software systems.
