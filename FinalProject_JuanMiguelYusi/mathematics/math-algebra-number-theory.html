Algebra & Number Theory

User: What is Algebra & Number Theory and why is it important?
Algebra studies symbols and rules for manipulating those symbols, forming equations and functions. Number theory explores properties of integers, divisibility, and prime numbers. Together, they form the backbone of many areas in cryptography, coding theory, and algorithm analysis.
