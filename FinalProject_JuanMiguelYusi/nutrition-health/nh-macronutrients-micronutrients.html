Macronutrients & Micronutrients

User: What are macronutrients and micronutrients?
Macronutrients (carbs, proteins, fats) provide energy and building blocks for the body. Micronutrients (vitamins, minerals) support metabolic functions and health. Balanced intake and variety are key to good nutrition.
