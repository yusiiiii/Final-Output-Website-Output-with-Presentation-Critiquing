Budgeting & Saving

User: How do I start budgeting and saving effectively?
Begin by tracking income and expenses, set realistic goals, create an emergency fund, and use the 50/30/20 rule (needs/wants/savings). Automate savings and reduce high-interest debt to improve long-term financial stability.
