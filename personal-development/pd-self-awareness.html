Self-Awareness

User: What is self-awareness and how can I improve it?
Self-awareness is recognizing your emotions, strengths, weaknesses, and values. Improve it by journaling, seeking feedback, practicing mindfulness, and reflecting on decisions to build better habits and relationships.
